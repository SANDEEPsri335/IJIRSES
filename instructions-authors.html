Instructions for Authors

General Guidelines

IJIRSES welcomes original research articles, review papers, short communications, and case studies. All submissions must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.

Manuscript Preparation

1 File Format

Submit manuscripts in Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) format. PDF submissions are not accepted.

2 Title Page

Include: Title (bold, title case), author names (full name with superscript numbers for affiliations), affiliations (department, institution, city, country), corresponding author email, ORCID IDs (optional).

3 Abstract

Provide a structured abstract of 250-300 words with sections: Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion. Include 4-6 keywords.

4 Main Text

Organize as: Introduction, Materials and Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgments, References. Use standard headings (bold, 14pt).

5 References

Use IEEE or APA style. Cite in text with numbers [1] or (author, year). List references alphabetically at the end.

6 Figures & Tables

Place figures/tables after references. Each with caption (above for tables, below for figures). Submit figures separately as JPEG/PNG (300 DPI).

Submission Checklist

  • Manuscript formatted according to guidelines
  • Cover letter with manuscript significance
  • Figures and tables with captions
  • Conflict of interest statement
  • Ethics approval (for human/animal studies)
