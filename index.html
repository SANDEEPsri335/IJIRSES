About the Journal

IJIRSES is a prestigious monthly open access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all disciplines of Sciences and Engineering. Our mission is to accelerate discovery by providing a rigorous yet rapid peer-review process and immediate, worldwide visibility.

Journal Highlights

Rapid Publication

Average 3–5 days from acceptance to publication, ensuring your research reaches the community quickly.

High Visibility

Articles indexed in leading databases worldwide, maximizing citation potential and research impact.

Rigorous Peer Review

Constructive review process with at least two independent experts ensuring scientific quality.

Multidisciplinary

Covering engineering, technology, natural sciences, and interdisciplinary research areas.

Open Access

Creative Commons licensing allows authors to share and adapt their work with proper credit.

The journal publishes original research articles, comprehensive reviews, and short communications that have not been previously published. All manuscripts undergo a thorough evaluation by the editorial board and external referees to ensure scientific soundness and novelty.

IJIRSES aims to be the platform where ideas from academia and industry converge to shape the future of science and technology.

We invite researchers, scientists, and practitioners to submit their innovative work and join our community of thought leaders.

Google Scholar Indexing

IJIRSES is indexed in Google Scholar. Track citations, h-index, and i10-index of our published articles. Our growing presence in academic databases ensures maximum visibility for your research.
