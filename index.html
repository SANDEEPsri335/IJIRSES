The journal publishes original research articles, comprehensive reviews, and short communications that have not been previously published. All manuscripts undergo a thorough evaluation by the editorial board and external referees to ensure scientific soundness and novelty.

IJIRSES aims to be the platform where ideas from academia and industry converge to shape the future of science and technology.

We invite researchers, scientists, and practitioners to submit their innovative work and join our community of thought leaders.
