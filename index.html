About the Journal

IJIRSES (International Journal of Innovative Research in Sciences and Engineering Studies) is an open-access, peer-reviewed international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research papers in the fields of science, engineering, and technology. We welcome original research articles, review papers, and case studies from researchers, scientists, and engineers worldwide.

Why Publish with IJIRSES?

  • Rapid peer review process
  • Open Access - freely available to all
  • DOI assignment for each paper
  • Indexed in major databases
  • No publication fee for first 50 submissions
  • Certificate of publication provided
  • Online submission and tracking
  • High visibility and citation potential
