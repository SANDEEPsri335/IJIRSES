About the Journal

Index Copernicus 2024: 92.45 NLM ID: 1023456789 Open access · monthly Peer-reviewed

IJIRSES is a prestigious monthly open access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all disciplines of Sciences and Engineering. Our mission is to accelerate discovery by providing a rigorous yet rapid peer-review process and immediate, worldwide visibility.

  • Rapid publication: average 30–45 days from submission to acceptance
  • High visibility: articles indexed in leading databases
  • Constructive peer review with at least two independent experts
  • No publication charges for authors (open access)
  • Global reach: read by researchers in 80+ countries
  • Editorial Tracking System for transparent processing
  • Multidisciplinary: engineering, technology, natural sciences
  • Creative Commons licensing – share and adapt with credit

The journal publishes original research articles, comprehensive reviews, and short communications that have not been previously published. All manuscripts undergo a thorough evaluation by the editorial board and external referees to ensure scientific soundness and novelty. IJIRSES aims to be the platform where ideas from academia and industry converge to shape the future of science and technology.

We invite researchers, scientists, and practitioners to submit their innovative work and join our community of thought leaders.
