About the Journal

IJIRSES is a prestigious monthly open access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all disciplines of Sciences and Engineering.

  • Rapid publication: average 3–5 days from acceptance to publication
  • High visibility: articles indexed in leading databases
  • Constructive peer review with at least two independent experts
  • Multidisciplinary: engineering, technology, natural sciences
  • Creative Commons licensing – share and adapt with credit

The journal publishes original research articles, comprehensive reviews, and short communications. All manuscripts undergo thorough evaluation by the editorial board and external referees.

We invite researchers to submit their innovative work.

Google Scholar — IJIRSES is indexed in Google Scholar. Track citations, h-index, and i10-index.
