Peer Review Process

Double-Blind Peer Review

IJIRSES follows a rigorous double-blind peer review process where both reviewers and authors remain anonymous. This ensures unbiased evaluation based solely on manuscript quality.

Review Timeline

Initial Submission
Day 0

Author submits manuscript through online system. Plagiarism check and format verification performed.

Editorial Assessment
Days 1-3

Editor-in-Chief assesses scope, quality, and adherence to guidelines. Desk rejection if不符合.

Reviewer Assignment
Days 3-7

At least 2 independent reviewers assigned based on expertise. Reviewers have 14 days to complete.

Review Completion
Days 14-21

Reviews collected. Editor makes initial decision based on recommendations.

Decision Communication
Days 21-25

Author notified of decision: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject.

Revision & Final Decision
Variable

Revised manuscripts re-reviewed. Final acceptance within 30-45 days average.

Review Criteria

  • Originality and novelty of research
  • Methodological rigor and validity
  • Clarity and quality of presentation
  • Significance to the field
  • Ethical compliance
