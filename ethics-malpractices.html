Publication Ethics & Malpractice Statement

IJIRSES follows the guidelines of the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) and adheres to the highest standards of publication ethics.

For Authors
  • Original work only – no plagiarism
  • Proper citation of sources
  • No duplicate submission
  • Authorship criteria met
  • Data availability statement
  • Conflict of interest disclosure
For Reviewers
  • Confidentiality of manuscripts
  • Objective and constructive feedback
  • Timely completion
  • Declare conflicts of interest
  • No personal criticism
For Editors
  • Fair and unbiased decisions
  • Confidentiality
  • Investigate ethical concerns
  • No conflict of interest
Publisher Responsibilities
  • Ensure editorial independence
  • Provide legal support if needed
  • Maintain archival records

Handling Misconduct

Any suspected ethical violation will be investigated according to COPE guidelines. Consequences may include:

  • Rejection of manuscript
  • Retraction of published article
  • Ban on future submissions
  • Reporting to institutions
COPE Member

IJIRSES is a member of COPE and follows its best practices.
