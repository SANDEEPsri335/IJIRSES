Aims & Scope

About the Journal

International Journal of Innovative Research in Sciences and Engineering Studies (IJIRSES) is an online open access monthly journal that publishes high-quality peer-reviewed research papers in all areas of sciences and engineering. The journal aims to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, engineers, and students to share and discuss new developments and innovations in their respective fields.

Aims

The primary aims of IJIRSES are:

  • To promote innovative research in sciences and engineering disciplines
  • To provide an open access platform for global knowledge exchange
  • To maintain high standards of peer review and publication ethics
  • To bridge the gap between academic research and industrial applications
  • To encourage interdisciplinary research collaborations
  • To support early career researchers and students

Scope

IJIRSES welcomes original research articles, review papers, short communications, and case studies in the following areas:

Engineering Sciences

  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Materials Science & Engineering
Sciences
  • Physics & Applied Physics

  • Biology & Biotechnology
  • Environmental Science
  • Earth Sciences

  • Data Science & Analytics
Interdisciplinary Areas
  • Nanotechnology
  • Renewable Energy
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
